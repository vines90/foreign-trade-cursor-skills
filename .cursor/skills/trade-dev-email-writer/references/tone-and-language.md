# 语气与措辞指南

## 我们要的语气：同行简报 / 顾问咨询 / 工厂代表的礼貌问询

不是销售员"全员喊单"。把自己想象成：

- 一个**有 20 年行业经验的工厂出口部代表**，
- 给一个**比你专业、且很忙的采购方**写一封简短私信，
- 目的是"被认真看一眼，并被回一句话"，不是"立刻拿单"。

## 5 个语气切换示范

| 销售员口吻（❌） | 同行简报口吻（✅） |
|---|---|
| `We are the leading manufacturer of jewellery boxes in China with 20+ years of experience and ISO9001 certified.` | `I run Long Term Pack, a Dongguan jewellery packaging factory (since 2005). Direct-from-factory, not a trader.` |
| `We can offer you the best price and the highest quality!` | `For matching SKUs, we typically save UK retailers 15–25% versus Alibaba intermediaries — happy to send a benchmark sheet.` |
| `Please buy from us, you will not regret!` | `Worth a quick comparison? No obligation either way.` |
| `Hope this email finds you well. My name is Yang and I am writing to introduce our company.` | `I'm reaching out because Arya already wholesales Velvet Boxes alongside the jewellery — that's exactly our core SKU range.` |
| `Looking forward to your reply!!! Best regards!!!` | `Either way, kind regards.` |

## 句式工具箱

### 开头（说原因）

- `I'm reaching out because <客户的具体动作/状态>.`
- `I noticed on your website that <客户的具体业务点>.`
- `Congratulations on <近期变化> — <说明为什么这是给你写信的好时机>.`
- `A respectful note, <一句关系定位> — <说明为什么直接联系本人>.`

### 中段（说为什么是你这家公司）

- `<Brand> already <做了什么具体的事>, which means <推断>.`
- `Most <同类客户> in your spot end up <一般做法>; in our experience, <我们的对应做法>.`
- `Two concrete things I can do this week: 1. … 2. …`
- `Two ways I would like to introduce ourselves, whichever fits your buying team's preference: 1. … 2. …`

### CTA（轻量、二选一、给台阶）

- `Worth a 15-min comparison?`
- `Either A. <动作1>, or B. <动作2> — whichever fits your team.`
- `If it's worth a quick call, happy to set one up.`
- `If packaging procurement now sits with someone else, a quick steer is appreciated.`
- `No obligation either way.`

### 结尾

- `Best regards,`
- `Kind regards,`
- `With kind regards,`（更正式，适合家族企业、长辈、英国老牌客户）
- `Either way, kind regards,`（带轻松感的结尾）
- 日文：`ご返信をお待ちしております。` / `ご検討のほど、よろしくお願い申し上げます。`

## 词汇替换表

| 营销词（❌） | 中性词（✅） |
|---|---|
| `cheap` / `cheapest` | `competitive` / `direct-from-factory` |
| `best price` | `benchmark price` / `factory pricing` |
| `huge discount` | （直接不用，引导对方索要 quote） |
| `amazing` / `incredible` / `world-class` | `consistent` / `reliable` / `solid` |
| `act now` / `hurry` | （直接不用） |
| `buy from us` | `work together` / `set up a small first order` |
| `we are the best` | `we focus on <某个具体细分>` |
| `100% guaranteed` | `if anything is off-spec, we replace at our cost`（具体动作） |
| `click here` | 直接给 URL，或写 `see <name> on our site` |
| `our company has been established for many years and has rich experience` | `<我方公司>, since <year>, focused on <细分>` |
| `please contact us if you are interested` | `if it's worth a comparison, happy to send a quick benchmark` |

## 7 个"立刻删掉"的句子

如果生成的草稿里出现下面任何一句，**立刻删掉重写**：

1. `Hope this email finds you well.`
2. `My name is X and I am writing to you from Y company.`
3. `We are a professional manufacturer of … with rich experience and competitive prices.`
4. `Looking forward to your reply.`
5. `Please feel free to contact us anytime.`
6. `We can satisfy any of your packaging needs.`
7. `Welcome to our website www…. for more information.`

## 关于"自我介绍"的正确写法

很多开发信问题出在第一段。常见错误：

```
❌ Hello, my name is Yang Li and I am the Overseas Sales Manager
   at Long Term Pack Limited, a leading jewellery packaging
   manufacturer in China with 20 years of experience. We supply
   premium quality boxes to many famous brands worldwide…
```

为什么不行：

- 通篇在说"我是谁、我多牛"
- 没说"为什么联系**你**"
- 一看就是模板群发

正确写法：

```
✅ Hi Eraj,

   Congratulations on stepping into the Director seat at Arya last
   September — packaging tends to be one of those line items worth
   a fresh look during a leadership transition, which is partly why
   I'm writing directly rather than relying on the intro I sent to
   info@ yesterday.
```

为什么好：

- 第一句就是"恭喜你 + 我是怎么知道的"
- 第二句直接说"为什么这个时机给你写"
- 隐含交代了"我已经发过 info@，但我重视你这个人"
- 全程没有自夸

## 不同客户类型的语气微调

| 客户类型 | 重点强调 | 避免 |
|---|---|---|
| 品牌方 / 零售 | 品牌呈现、unboxing 体验、礼赠场景、店内陈列 | 谈"OEM"、"代工"（对方可能反感） |
| 渠道商 / 批发商 | margin、benchmark、库存周转、SKU 替代 | 谈"高端定制"（对方要的是稳定标品） |
| 制造商 / 同行 | 产能补位、特殊工艺合作、material option | 谈"我比你便宜"（对方就是工厂） |
| 礼赠服务商 | 季节项目、批量打包、品牌礼盒、企业客户 | 谈"零售包装"（场景错位） |
| 家族企业 / 老字号 | 长期主义、世代传承、低调可靠 | 美式热情口吻、emoji、销售话术 |
| 日本客户 | 敬语、谦虚、节制、不打扰、明确节奏 | 直接谈价、过快推 CTA、过度热情 |

## 关于"说服"的根本观点

不要试图在一封邮件里说服对方下单。一封首封开发信的成功定义是：

- 对方读完后**愿意回复一句话**
- 对方读完后**记得"哦这家不像群发"**

只要做到这两点，你已经在 99% 的开发信里属于上游 5%。
